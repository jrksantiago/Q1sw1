 CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Capitan Tiago A rich Filipino that is short, fat and round faced. He is to be known as the richest man in Binundok and gained his riches from the land that he owns in Pampanga and Laguna he walks with a cane with a cigarrete.
Don Rafael Filipino who got arrested and sent to prison for murdering someone who has spanish blood. He shortly died in prison before his death no one would want to be his lawyer to defend him against his crime of murdering a spanish since they didnt want to get involved. He has a son named Crisostomo.
Crisostomo Ibarra Got back from Europa he is very friendly in greeting people his father is friends with Capitan Tiago. His father is Don Rafael who died in prison.
Padre Damaso A Spanish friar he is very rough and talks powerfully. Very friendly to the Ibarra Family and has pure spanish blood.
Padre Sibyla A Dominikano in Binundok he talks very shortly and became a professor in San Juan de Letran. He gets sick easily and he is the nicest prayle.
